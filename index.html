    4. Guides And Conventions
      • Terminology

        Guide (GDE): A document that offers information or instruction; guidebook.

        Convention (CONV): A generally accepted principle, method, or behavior emphasized by HealthStream management. Similar to a Policy.

        Best Practice (BP): How an individual team implements a convention or policy. A method or technique that has been generally accepted as superior to alternatives because it produces superior results versus those achieved by other means or because it has become a standard way of doing things. Implements a convention or standard.

        Standard Operating Procedure (SOP): Guide, Procedure that may or may not be best practice. Ex. A DBA SOP for Developers but is not best practice.

        Policy (POL): A statement of intent implemented as a procedure or protocol as adopted by a governance body, e.g. HealthStream, SOX, GDPR, CCPA, etc.

        HowTo (HOW): Something, such as a book or learning situation, that provides practical advice and detailed instruction in an activity.

        Template (TMPL): An electronic file with a predesigned, customized format and structure ready to be filled in.
